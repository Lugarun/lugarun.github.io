My brothers skate skies on the Ottawa River

Hello all, I’m Lukas. I am constantly stuck between the desire to write a text editor, terminal multiplexor, markup language, Haskell machine learning library, NixOs configuration. When I’ve managed to tear myself away from these obsessions.. um hobbies, I work on hardware validation at the University of Waterloo.

I am an avid reader of blogs by people like Xe Iaso, Chris Penner, Dillon Chan, and Japorized. This site is my piece of the blogosphere that I love so much.
